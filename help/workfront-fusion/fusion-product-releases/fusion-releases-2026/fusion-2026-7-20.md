This page describes all enhancements made in Adobe Workfront Fusion the week of July 20, 2026.

For a list of all recent changes, see [Adobe Workfront Fusion release activity](/help/workfront-fusion/fusion-product-releases/fusion-release-activity.md).

For a list of recent bug fixes in Workfront Fusion, see the [Workfront Maintenance Updates](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/workfront-known-issues/releases/current-updates) page and check for any updates labeled Workfront Fusion Maintenance Update.

## Adobe Express connector and modules now available

You can now use Workfront Fusion to connect to your Adobe Express account.

With the Adobe Express modules, you can:

* Export a rendition of a document as an image, video, or PDF
* Generate a variation of a document based on provided input parameters
* Retrieve a list of tagged documents and their metadata
* Retrieve details about a document's pages and tagged elements
* Retrieve the status of a job by its ID

For more information, see [Adobe Express modules](/help/workfront-fusion/references/apps-and-modules/adobe-connectors/adobe-express-modules.md).
