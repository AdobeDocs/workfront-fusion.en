# Workfront Fusion release activity: Week of May 4, 2026

This page describes all enhancements made in Adobe Workfront Fusion the week of May 4, 2026.

For a list of all recent changes, see [Adobe Workfront Fusion release activity](/help/workfront-fusion/fusion-product-releases/fusion-release-activity.md).

For a list of recent bug fixes in Workfront Fusion, see the [Workfront Maintenance Updates](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/workfront-known-issues/releases/current-updates) page and check for any updates labeled Workfront Fusion Maintenance Update.


## New functions available in the mapping panel

To give you more flexibility when mapping data in your scenarios, we've added several new functions to the mapping panel. These additions expand what you can do with general, date and time, string, math, and array functions when transforming and manipulating data.

For a complete list of available functions, see [Functions references: article index](/help/workfront-fusion/references/mapping-panel/functions/functions-toc.md).


## New Firefly Audio and Video connector

To expand Fusion's capabilities for using Firefly with media, we've created the Adobe Firefly Audio and Video connector. With the Adobe Firefly Audio and Video modules, you can:

* Describe or render templates
* Dub audio or video
* Generate speech from text, or generate avatar videos from text or audio
* Reframe videos
* Transcribe audio or video

For information and instructions, see [Adobe Firefly Audio and Video modules](/help/workfront-fusion/references/apps-and-modules/adobe-connectors/adobe-firefly-audio-video.md).



## New Firefly Lightroom connector

To expand Fusion's capabilities for using Firefly with Adobe Lightroom, we've created the Adobe Firefly Lightroom connector. With the Adobe Firefly Lightroom modules, you can:

* Add XMP to an image
* Apply Lightroom edits or presets
* Auto-straighten or auto-tone images

For information and instructions, see [Adobe Lightroom modules](/help/workfront-fusion/references/apps-and-modules/adobe-connectors/adobe-firefly-audio-video.md).



## Updates to Adobe Substance connector

We've added some new options to modules in the Adobe Substance connector:

* Generate 3D object: Added custom model options
* Create 3D scene: Added USDC as an encoding option
* Render 3d object: Added algorithm, export distance to camera, and export matte options
* Render 3d object (basic version): Added algorithm, export distance to camera, and export matte options

For information and instructions for these modules, see [Adobe Substance modules](/help/workfront-fusion/references/apps-and-modules/adobe-connectors/adobe-substance-modules.md).
