## New Firefly Audio and Video connector

To expand Fusion's capabilities for using Firefly with media, we've created the Adobe Firefly Audio and Video connector. With the Adobe Firefly Audio and Video connector, you can:

* Describe or render templates
* Dub audio or video
* Generate speech from text, or generate avatar videos from text or audio
* Reframe videos
* Transcribe audio or video

For information and instructions, see [Adobe Firefly Audio and Video modules]
