# Workfront Fusion release activity: Week of May 11, 2026

This page describes all enhancements made in Adobe Workfront Fusion the week of May 11, 2026.

For a list of all recent changes, see [Adobe Workfront Fusion release activity](/help/workfront-fusion/fusion-product-releases/fusion-release-activity.md).

For a list of recent bug fixes in Workfront Fusion, see the [Workfront Maintenance Updates](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/workfront-known-issues/releases/current-updates) page and check for any updates labeled Workfront Fusion Maintenance Update.

## New modules availabe in the Firefly connector

To expand the capabilities of the Adobe Firefly connector, we've added the following new modules:

* Generate images with Image5
* Generate video
* Generate precise composite
* Generate adaptive composite

For more information, see [Adobe Firefly modules](/help/workfront-fusion/references/apps-and-modules/adobe-connectors/adobe-firefly-modules.md).
