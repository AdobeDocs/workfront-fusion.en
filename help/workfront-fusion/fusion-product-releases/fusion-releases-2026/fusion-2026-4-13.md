# Workfront Fusion release activity: Week of April 13, 2026

This page describes all enhancements made in Adobe Workfront Fusion the week of April 13, 2026.

For a list of all recent changes, see [Adobe Workfront Fusion release activity](/help/workfront-fusion/fusion-product-releases/fusion-release-activity.md).

For a list of recent bug fixes in Workfront Fusion, see the [Workfront Maintenance Updates](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/workfront-known-issues/releases/current-updates) page and check for any updates labeled Workfront Fusion Maintenance Update.

## New chained scenario relationships area

To make your chained scenarios easier to see and manage, we've added the Relationships area to the scenario details page. In the Relationships area, you can:

* View a map of relationships between chained scenarios
* See diagrams of each scenario 
* Go directly to any scenario in the chain

For information and instructions, see [View and manage chained scenario relationships](/help/workfront-fusion/manage-scenarios/view-chained-scenario-relationships.md).
